Every LEGO set retiring in 2024 – December update

08/12/2024 09/12/2024 Jack Yates 535 Views 0 Comments

It ’s the final month of 2024 and time is nearly up for hundreds of retiring LEGO sets as stock is starting to run dry, or has already in some cases.

The life cycle of LEGO sets continues month by month and it is no more evident any time of year than it is right now. Following the reveal of dozens of upcoming 2025 models, the new arrivals next month don ’t change the fact that hundreds of LEGO sets across almost all themes are about to retire for good.

The likes of LEGO Indiana Jones are about to retire completely, at least for now, as well and here ’s your final monthly update for the list of retiring LEGO sets for 2024 as a new year looms on the horizon. While these dates are always subject to change, almost all of them have been officially confirmed by the LEGO Group before the dates arrive in less than one month. Click here to keep track of sets retiring beyond 2024 on our dedicated page.

LEGO Star Wars retiring sets 2024

Wave goodbye to these LEGO Star Wars after the Black Friday sales, which saw the stock of 75313 AT-AT finally run out worldwide.

LEGO Ideas retiring sets 2024

The following LEGO Ideas sets are about to retire and a few of them are discounted in LEGO stores and on LEGO.com right now as double points continue to be offered.

LEGO Marvel retiring sets 2024

From 76218 Sanctum Sanctorum to 10782 Hulk vs. Rhino Truck Showdown , this is your last call to grab some retiring LEGO Marvel sets wherever stock is still available.

LEGO Harry Potter retiring sets 2024

The Black Friday and LEGO Insiders Weekend sales are done and here ’s hoping you grabbed the LEGO Harry Potter sets you needed before they finally retire at the end of this month.

LEGO Indiana Jones retiring sets 2024

LEGO Indiana Jones is going to be over soon as the theme ’s three builds are all about to retire for good, including 77015 Temple of the Golden Idol .

LEGO Technic retiring sets 2024

It ’s nearly curtains for these retiring LEGO Technic sets as the theme is about to say goodbye to several sets, including 42152 Firefighter Aircraft after worldwide sales during Black Friday .

LEGO Icons retiring sets 2024

Hopefully, you ’ve managed to pick up the retiring LEGO Icons sets you still want to collect as following sales on several models during the LEGO Insiders Weekend as stock is starting to run out on the following models.

LEGO Architecture retiring sets 2024

After a year of the same selection, LEGO Architecture ’s retiring sets for 2024 remain unchanged one last time as we near another year of the theme.

LEGO NINJAGO retiring sets 2024

The following LEGO NINJAGO models officially retire this month as another season of the theme is right around the corner, including new minifigures and elements to collect.

LEGO DREAMZzz retiring sets 2024

LEGO DREAMZzz is confirmed to return in 2025 but in the meantime, keep an eye out for these retiring sets one last time in stores and online.

LEGO Batman / DC Super Heroes retiring sets 2024

As 76252 Batcave – Shadow Box sells out, time is running out to grab these remaining retiring LEGO Batman builds before the theme remembers characters outside of Batman ’s corner of the DC universe in 2025.

LEGO Super Mario retiring sets 2024

It ’s the final month to pick up these retiring LEGO Super Mario models as stock for some of the most desirable entries has run out after Black Friday , including 71374 Nintendo Entertainment System

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog retiring sets 2024

With the third Sonic the Hedgehog movie set to release later this month, it ’s the most appropriate and last chance to grab the four retiring LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog sets where stock is still available.

LEGO Creator retiring sets 2024

LEGO Creator 31141 Main Street remains available with a 40% discount in some regions as part of the many LEGO Creator sets retiring at the end of this month.

LEGO Speed Champions retiring sets 2024

This is your last call to pick up one or more of the many retiring LEGO Speed Champions builds before the end of the month, including a duo of double packs.

LEGO Collectible Minifigures retiring sets 2024

Finish collecting the LEGO Dungeons &Dragons minifigures before they finally retire at the end of 2024, which is in less than a month.

LEGO Disney retiring sets 2024

After a bevvy of sales during Black Friday and the LEGO Insiders Weekend, there ’s still a chance to pick up the retiring LEGO Wish sets with a heavy discount, including the detailed 43224 King Magnifico ’s Castle .

LEGO Jurassic World retiring sets 2024

After years of waiting for a LEGO Jurassic Park Visitor Centre model, time is running out to track down a copy of the long-awaited 76961 Visitor Center: T. rex &Raptor Attack before its retirement in a few weeks.

LEGO Monkie Kid retiring sets 2024

LEGO Monkie Kid isn ’t over with more models planned for 2025, but it ’s not immune to the passage of time either and many builds are soon to retire from factories and shelves accordingly.

LEGO City retiring sets 2024

From the biggest build in LEGO City history to the usual mix of vehicles, trains and locations, here ’s what ’s retiring from LEGO City this month, now including 60362 Car Wash and 60363 Ice-Cream Shop .

LEGO Art retiring sets 2024

The days are numbered for these LEGO Art sets, including 31210 Modern Art which is still on sale after Black Friday on LEGO.com .

LEGO Friends retiring sets 2024

Many models from Heartlake City will soon retire and here ’s exactly what is leaving the active LEGO portfolio at the end of this month from LEGO Friends .

LEGO Gabby ’s Dollhouse retiring sets 2024

Now that How to Train Your Dragon is rumoured to be a single set, Gabby ’s Dollhouse may remain the only LEGO Dreamworks theme currently available, despite three of its sets retiring this month.

LEGO BrickHeadz retiring sets 2024

There ’s never been a better time to grab 40672 Sonic the Hedgehog: Knuckles &Shadow as the third Sonic film featuring the pair will debut this month right before the BrickHeadz retire.

LEGO Minecraft retiring sets 2024

21178 The Fox Lodge narrowly avoids the December 2024 retirement window, but that ’s not the case for the many other LEGO Minecraft models leaving shelves and factories this month.

LEGO Seasonal retiring sets 2024

As the LEGO Chinese Traditional Festivals 2024 sets sell out, there ’s still time to grab some of the other LEGO Seasonal models retiring at the end of December 2024.

LEGO Classic retiring sets 2024

A familiar selection of element-filled boxes is still up for retirement one last time as the following LEGO Classic sets are retiring in less than one month.

LEGO exclusive sets retiring in 2024

The following LEGO store -exclusive sets are retiring soon in case you need any stocking stuffers or discounted models just before Christmas.
