Every LEGO set retiring in 2025 – January update

06/01/202507/01/2025 Jack Yates 1222 Views 0 Comments

Another year has begun and that means a fresh list of retiring LEGO sets to look through, with hundreds of models leaving shelves this year. Here’s a complete round-up of them all…

Following the retirement of hundreds of sets in December 2024, it’s time for another year of tracking which sets are leaving this year and when. For the unfamiliar, every LEGO set has an eventual retirement date by which it will no longer be produced, and after which it will eventually run out of stock for good. In some cases, LEGO sets can be re-released but this is exceedingly rare.

Some of the sets in this list are marked with a ‘New’ label, which indicates movement between years as shown more thoroughly in our extensive list of every LEGO set retiring in 2025 and beyond. For this list, they note sets that have had their retirement dates moved to 2025, instead of 2026 or beyond. Here’s a closer look at what’s currently retiring in 2025 but remember that all of these dates are subject to change and this list is certain to look much different in December.

LEGO Star Wars retiring sets 2025

For LEGO Star Wars enthusiasts, make sure you pick up 75304 Darth Vader Helmet, 75394 Imperial Star Destroyer and the many other models in the following table before the end of the year.

LEGO Ideas retiring sets 2025

Over at LEGO Ideas, it’s going to be a busy year for retiring sets with major builds from 21335 Motorised Lighthouse to 21344 The Orient Express Train all slated to leave production.

LEGO Marvel retiring sets 2025

LEGO Marvel fans may want to prioritise 76178 Daily Bugle in 2025 as the modular Marvel model is finally set to retire later this year, following 76218 Sanctum Sanctorum in 2024. This set is even more rife with exclusive minifigures, characters and Easter eggs.

LEGO Harry Potter retiring sets 2025

Both 75978 Diagon Alley and 76391 Hogwarts Icons Collectors’ Edition are up for retirement at the end of 2025 as time seems to be up for the huge LEGO Harry Potter builds.

LEGO Technic retiring sets 2025

LEGO Technic’s assortment of complicated creations include some notable sets retiring later this year, from 42115 Lamborghini and the huge 42146 Liebherr Crawler Crane LR 13000 to the uniquely space-centric 42180 Mars Crew Exploration Rover.

LEGO Icons retiring sets 2025

Whether you long for the glory days of LEGO Castle or are building up your collection of modular buildings, there’s likely to be a retiring LEGO Icons set that will interest you before 2025 is through.

LEGO Architecture retiring sets 2025

Two of the remaining LEGO Architecture skylines are retiring in 2025, including the longstanding 21028 New York City and 21044 Paris.

LEGO Art retiring sets 2025

LEGO Art’s retiring set selection for 2025 includes the iconic 31213 Mona Lisa – giving it a shelf life of just over a year – and 31208 Hokusai – The Great Wave, complete with its varied techniques to recreate the woodblock print.

LEGO NINJAGO retiring sets 2025

Over in the land of NINJAGO, don’t miss out on 71814 Tournament Temple City and the exclusive minifigures within before the end of 2025. Before then though, 71815 Kai’s Source Dragon Battle is currently retiring in July.

LEGO DREAMZzz retiring sets 2025

In the world of LEGO DREAMZzz, all remaining sets in the theme – including the sets that have only just launched – are up for retirement at the end of the year.

LEGO Batman / DC Super Heroes retiring sets 2025

LEGO DC has finally branched out from Batman again for 2025, but 76302 Superman Mech vs. Lex Luthor is only sticking around for one year and retires in December at the time of writing.

LEGO Super Mario retiring sets 2025

Of the LEGO Super Mario sets retiring later this year, 71431 Bowser’s Muscle Car may be tempting to add to the Mario Kart range and it retires sooner than the likes of 71411 The Mighty Bowser and 71426 Piranha Plant.

July 31, 2025

December 31, 2025

LEGO Animal Crossing retiring sets 2025

Seven LEGO Animal Crossing sets are due to retire at the end of 2025, including iconic locations such as 77050 Nook’s Cranny & Rosie’s House and 77052 K.K.’s Concert at the Plaza.

LEGO Fortnite retiring sets 2025

All but one LEGO Fortnite set is currently due to retire this year, ranging from 77070 Durrr Burger to 77072 Peely Bone. That will leave 77073 Battle Bus alone on shelves (or in the company of whatever else might come out in 2025).

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog retiring sets 2025

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog fans will want to try to grab 76998 Knuckles and the Master Emerald Shrine before the end of 2025 since it’s up for retirement and includes the only minifigure of Rouge to date.

LEGO Creator retiring sets 2025

The first priority for LEGO Creator sets in 2025 should be 31139 Cosy House as it’s up for retirement in July, before a much larger selection of sets leaves shelves later this year – including the long-running 31109 Pirate Ship.

July 31, 2025

December 31, 2025

LEGO Despicable Me retiring sets 2025

It looks to be curtains for LEGO Despicable Me this year as everything in the animated theme is due to retire in 2025. 75583 Minions and Gru’s Family Mansion may be worth picking up for the minifigures but 75582 Brick-Built Gru and Minions makes for an interesting display too.

LEGO Wicked retiring sets 2025

Two of the three newly-released LEGO Wicked sets are due to retire by the end of 2025, so best make a beeline for these if you’ve enjoyed part one of the two-part silver-screen adaptation.

LEGO Wednesday retiring sets 2025

Both LEGO Wednesday’s non-BrickHeadz sets (but also its BrickHeadz set – scroll down for that) are retiring in 2025, giving them just over a year in production. This mini-doll line will be short-lived if things don’t change.

LEGO Speed Champions retiring sets 2025

Perhaps unsurprisingly, 76919 2023 McLaren Formula 1 Car – which will be usurped by a newer version of the same car in March 2025, as part of LEGO Speed Champions’ entire Formula 1 starting grid – is due to retire in July, before seven more sets race off into the sunset in December.

July 31, 2025

December 31, 2025

LEGO Collectible Minifigures retiring sets 2025

LEGO Collectible Minifigures series generally enjoy just four months on shelves, which means you’ve only got until the next batch drops to collect the ones you want. Leftover stock may be floating around at third-party retailers for a little while after, but that probably doesn’t extend to Wolfpack Beastmasters for 71048 Series 27…

April 30, 2025

August 31, 2025

LEGO Disney retiring sets 2025

LEGO Disney is another theme with split retirement dates in 2025, with three products due to leave shelves in July before a wider selection retires in December. 43222 Disney Castle is the one to save up for…

July 31, 2025

December 31, 2025

LEGO Jurassic World retiring sets 2025

Five LEGO Jurassic World sets are up for retirement in 2025, including the first in what now seems to be an annual series of brick-built fossils.

LEGO City retiring sets 2025

LEGO City’s high turnover rate of sets will continue as expected in 2025, with plenty of this year’s releases only staying in production for 12 months at most. There are some heavy hitters from 2024 in here too, though – including 60409 Yellow Mobile Construction Crane and 60434 Space Base and Rocket Launchpad.

LEGO Friends retiring sets 2025

Like LEGO City, LEGO Friends will see a vast number of sets leave shelves in 2025, including at least 11 sets that have only debuted this year. That’s provided nothing shuffles around between now and December, of course…

LEGO BrickHeadz retiring sets 2025

LEGO BrickHeadz collectors should take note of the characters heading for the great statuette retirement home in the sky in 2025, among which are a handful of recent releases and (at last) 2022’s 40547 Obi-Wan Kenobi & Darth Vader.

LEGO Minecraft retiring sets 2025

LEGO Minecraft’s first 18+ set is currently slated to enjoy less than 18 months on shelves, having debuted in August 2024 and with a scheduled retirement date of December 2025. Best push that one to the top of your list if you haven’t already.

LEGO Seasonal retiring sets 2025

A single LEGO Seasonal set is on the way out in July, followed by several more in December – including both of this year’s main Lunar New Year sets and last year’s bonus Christmas build 40746 Santa’s Delivery Truck.

July 31, 2025

December 31, 2025

LEGO Classic retiring sets 2025

Just three LEGO Classic sets are retiring in 2025, split between July and December. These are always a good shout if you’re just after a bunch of basic bricks to piece together your own creations.

July 31, 2025

December 31, 2025

LEGO exclusive sets retiring in 2025

Two LEGO sets that seem to be free of parent themes are retiring in 2025, and given the wider reception to this pair it’s perhaps not surprising to see them gone so soon.
